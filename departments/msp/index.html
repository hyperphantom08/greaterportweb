Maine State Police

The Maine State Police serves as the primary state law enforcement agency, providing patrol services, criminal investigations, and specialized units across Maine.

  • State-wide law enforcement authority
  • Interstate highway system
  • Rural and unincorporated areas
  • Assistance to local departments
  • State government facility security
